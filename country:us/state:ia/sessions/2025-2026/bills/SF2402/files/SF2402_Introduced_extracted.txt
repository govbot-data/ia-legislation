This bill amends or enacts provisions relating 29 to different types of business entities existing under the 30 jurisdiction of the secretary of state (secretary), and 31 specifically the secretaryâs filing office. A business 32 entity is composed of one or more persons holding a legal 33 interest in a for-profit business entity, cooperative interest 34 in a cooperative association, or beneficial interest in a 35 -36- LSB 5534SV (1) 91 da/jh 36/ 38 S.F. 2402 nonprofit business entity, with the interest holders pooling 1 capital, and being shielded from personal liability from the 2 acts or omissions of the business entity.
