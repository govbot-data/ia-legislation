The new tax rates on 23 the taxable funds of an HMO in the bill are as follows: for 24 the period in calendar year 2026 beginning January 1, 2026, 25 and ending September 30, 2026, 3.5 percent; for the period 26 in calendar year 2026 beginning on or after October 1, 2026, 27 and ending December 31, 2026, .95 percent; and for the 2027 28 calendar year and subsequent calendar years, the rate remains 29 at .95 percent. 30 Currently, the premium tax is due March 1 of the year 31 following the calendar year for which the tax is due; however, 32 one-half of the premium taxes are required to be prepaid on 33 June 1 and one-half on August 15.
