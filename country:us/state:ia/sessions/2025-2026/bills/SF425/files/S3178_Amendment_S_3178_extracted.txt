4 <Section 1. Section 558A.1, Code 2025, is amended by adding
5 the following new subsections:
6 NEW SUBSECTION. 3A. “Lead service line” means a service
7 line which is made of lead. A galvanized service line is
8 considered a lead service line if the line ever was or is
9 currently downstream of any lead service line or service line
10 of unknown material. For the purposes of this subsection,
11 “downstream” means the section of the service line between a
12 lead service line and the water meter.
13 NEW SUBSECTION. 4A. “Service line” means the pipe
14 connecting the water main to the interior plumbing in a
15 building. For the purposes of this subsection, “service line”
16 includes the pipe from the water main to the water meter, not
17 including interior plumbing.
18 Sec. 2. Section 558A.4, subsection 1, paragraph a, Code
19 2025, is amended to read as follows:
20 a. The disclosure statement shall include information

23 significant defects in the structural integrity of the
24 structure and the presence of lead service lines, as provided
25 in rules which shall be adopted by the real estate commission
26 pursuant to section 543B.9. The rules may require the
27 disclosure to include information relating to the property’s
28 zoning classification; the condition of plumbing, heating, or
29 electrical systems; or the presence of pests.
