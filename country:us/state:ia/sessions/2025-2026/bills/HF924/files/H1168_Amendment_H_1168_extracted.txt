4 INCOME TAX CREDIT FOR FIREARM SAFETY DEVICES
5 Section 1. NEW SECTION. 422.10C Firearm safety device tax
6 credit.
7 1. The taxes imposed under this subchapter, less the credits
8 allowed under section 422.12, shall be reduced by a firearm
9 safety device tax credit equal to the sales price of a firearm
10 safety device purchased during the tax year by the taxpayer,
11 not to exceed five hundred dollars.
12 2. For purposes of this section:
13 a. “Firearm safety device” means a device designed
14 to prevent a firearm from being operated without first
15 deactivating the device or a qualified gun safe.
16 b. “Qualified gun safe” means a safe specifically
17 manufactured to store firearms and constructed of steel or a
18 material of equal or greater strength, which safe is purchased
19 new by the taxpayer from a firearms dealer licensed under
20 federal law, or a retailer as defined in section 423.1, and
21 which safe is purchased for the personal, noncommercial use of
22 the taxpayer.
23 c. “Sales price” means the same as defined in section 423.1.
24 3. Any credit in excess of the taxpayer’s tax liability for
25 the tax year is not refundable but may be credited to the tax
26 liability for the following year.
27 Sec. ___. APPLICABILITY. This division of this Act applies
28 to firearm safety devices purchased on or after the effective
29 date of this division of this Act.
30 Sec. ___. RETROACTIVE APPLICABILITY. This division of this
31 Act applies retroactively to January 1, 2025, for tax years
32 beginning on or after that date.
