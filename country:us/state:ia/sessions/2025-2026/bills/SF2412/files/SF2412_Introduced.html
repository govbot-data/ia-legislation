The bill requires professional licensing 29 boards to use the SAVE program clearinghouse to verify the 30 U.S. citizenship and immigration status of an individual who 31 has applied for a professional license from the board and to 32 decline to issue or renew a license if the licensing board is 33 unable to verify that the person is lawfully present in the 34 United States.
