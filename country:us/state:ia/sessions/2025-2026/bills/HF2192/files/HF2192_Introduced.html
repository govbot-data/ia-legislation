H.F. 2192 This bill makes changes relating to election requirements 1 and limitations for the issuance of bonds by a school district. 2 The bill requires that a proposition to authorize the 3 issuance of bonds by a school district receive the affirmative 4 vote of at least 80 percent of the total votes cast for and 5 against the proposition at the election. The bill retains 6 the existing requirement that propositions to authorize the 7 issuance of bonds by a county, city, school corporation that 8 is not a school district, township, or other local board or 9 commission receive the affirmative vote of at least 60 percent 10 of the total votes cast for and against the proposition. 11 Under the bill, if a school district submits a bond 12 proposition under Code section 75.1 that fails to gain approval 13 of the voters, the school district is then prohibited from 14 submitting another bond proposal to voters for a period of four 15 years. 16 As a condition of and prior to calling an election for the 17 issuance of bonds under Code chapter 296, the bill requires the 18 school district to designate and deposit in one or more funds 19 of the school district an amount equal to or greater than 50 20 percent of the total cost of the project or purpose for which 21 the bonds are proposed to be issued. The amount so deposited 22 and designated shall be used solely for the project or purpose 23 unless the proposition of issuing the bonds fails at election 24 or the proposal to issue bonds is abandoned by the school 25 board. 26 The bill makes a corresponding change in Code section 296.6. 27 -3- LSB 5527YH (4) 91 mb/md 3/ 3
