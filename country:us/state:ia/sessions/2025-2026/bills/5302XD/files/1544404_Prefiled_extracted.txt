16 EXPLANATION
17 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
18 the explanation’s substance by the members of the general assembly.
19 This bill strikes a prohibition on the Iowa public
20 employees’ retirement system (IPERS) requiring electronic
21 records and electronic signatures of members to the extent
22 permitted under Code chapter 554D (electronic transactions ——
23 computer agreements). The bill also allows IPERS to release
24 the legal name of a member or beneficiary to the treasurer of
25 state to assist IPERS in locating the member or beneficiary.
26 The bill removes the requirement for IPERS, between January
27 and June of each calendar year, to provide each member with a
28 statement of the member’s accumulated contributions and benefit
29 credits accrued up to the end of the prior calendar year and
30 instead requires IPERS to provide each member a statement
31 reflecting the prior four quarters during each calendar year.
32 Under current law, a retired member can receive monthly
33 retirement allowance payments via electronic funds transfers
34 or, with the cost of an administrative fee, as paper warrants.
35 The bill provides a third option, allowing a retired member to
