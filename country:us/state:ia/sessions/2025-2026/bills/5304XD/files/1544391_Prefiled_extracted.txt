4 EXPLANATION
5 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
6 the explanation’s substance by the members of the general assembly.
7 This bill repeals Code section 37A.1, which makes the
8 sale, trade, or transfer of veterans commemorative property
9 on a person’s property a simple misdemeanor if done without
10 the department of veterans affairs’ authorization, creates
11 criteria for the department to use in granting authorization,
12 and gives the department rulemaking authority to administer
13 the Code section. The bill also strikes a corresponding
14 provision requiring the department to authorize a sale, trade,
15 or transfer of veterans commemorative property pursuant to Code
16 section 37A.1.
