H.F. 2551 This bill limits the amount a landlord can increase rent 1 on current tenants under residential landlord and tenant 2 agreements and in manufactured home communities or mobile 3 home parks. The bill provides that if a landlord applies a 4 rent increase to the dwelling unit or mobile home space of a 5 current tenant, the percentage increase shall not be greater 6 than either the percentage increase of the consumer price index 7 for all urban consumers in the midwest region over the previous 8 12 months or the percentage increase of the property value of 9 the premises or the manufactured home community or mobile home 10 park, respectively, as determined by the county assessor for 11 the same period since the last rent increase, whichever is 12 higher. 13 Under current law, a tenant of a mobile home space must 14 be notified in writing of any rent increase at least 90 days 15 before the effective date of the rent increase. The bill 16 requires tenants in dwelling units to be notified of a rent 17 increase in the same manner. 18 -2- LSB 5708YH (3) 91 lh/jh 2/ 2
