9 EXPLANATION
10 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
11 the explanation’s substance by the members of the general assembly.
12 This bill relates to the appointment of counsel by the court
13 for an indigent person.
14 Current law provides that if no contract attorney is
15 available for appointment by the court, the court may appoint
16 a noncontract attorney.
17 The bill provides that if the court determines that no
18 contract attorney is available to represent the person, the
19 court is required to appoint a noncontract attorney.
20 The bill takes effect upon enactment.
